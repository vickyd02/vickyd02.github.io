Currently, I am a Stanford graduate student studying energy sciences and engineering. I am working on researching the planning processes for decarbonized, reliable, and resilient electricity systems. Specifically, I am focused on developing energy optimization, resource adequacy, and transmission planning models that emphasize cost and resource optimization while also promoting grid reliability and stability. These models can support policy analysis, capacity expansion and resource planning, and renewables integration.

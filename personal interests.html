Personal Interests

Adventure and Travel

I’m deeply passionate about slow, intentional travel. I love journeys by foot, thumb, train, or boat—anything that helps me understand a place from the ground up. I’ve crossed borders on foot, hitched rides across Europe, and spent weeks in the wilderness.

❮ ❯

Backpacking and Hiking

I love the solitude and joy of long-distance hiking. Whether it's the California Sierras or the Julian Alps, I find peace in sleeping under the stars and waking up to a world without walls.

❮ ❯

Climbing and Mountaineering

I enjoy pushing my limits and working through mental puzzles on rock and ice. Climbing brings together discipline, creativity, and a deep connection with the landscape.

❮ ❯

Running

Running clears my mind and centers my day. I often run at dawn, watching the world wake up. I love running marathons for causes I care about.
